Who are we?


FEWZ Media Africa is a leading player in the highly competitive PR and Project management industry. We have unmatched skills in a vast arrays of media and communication competencies.
We employ a network of specialists in delivering unrivalled discretionary services for institutional entities and high network individuals.
We focus on using inspiration, empowerment and action to help brands and organizations connect with their audiences, enhance their bottom-lines, and lead in their industries.
We boast strong thought leadership in the PR industry, supported by a global network of academic and industry professionals. This means that through us you can access emerging best practices and innovations shaping the global and Nigerian PR industry.
We have 6 major practice areas: Corporate Practice, Technology Practice, SME Practice, Governance Practice, Faith Practice and the International Practice – with work for brands across Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya and South Africa.
As Africa’s leading core digital communication platform, we have access to key indices that will turn around opportunities into access to core successful areas.
